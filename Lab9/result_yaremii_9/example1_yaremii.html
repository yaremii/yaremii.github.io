Hamster

Hamsters are small rodents that are commonly kept as house pets. They are distinguishable from other rodents due to their short tails, stubby legs and small ears. Hamsters have many different colors, including black, grey, brown, white, yellow, red or a mixture of several colors.

Dog

The dog is one of the most popular pets in the world (of course cat lovers will argue for cats). Dogs have long played an important role in the lives of humans. It's thought that dogs have been pets for thousands of years. Dogs are often called Man's Best Friend. This is because dogs help man out in so many ways.

Cat

Cats conserve energy by sleeping for an average of 13 to14 hours a day. Cats have flexible bodies and teeth adapted for hunting small animals such as mice and rats. There are lots of types, or breeds, of cats. Cats come in many different colors, sizes, with hair, without hair, and with or without tails. Some of the most popular breeds include the Maine Coon, the Persian, the Siamese, and the American Shorthair.

Parrot

Parrots have curved bills (beaks), strong legs and clawed feet. Parrots are often brightly coloured. Parrots are believed to be one of the most intelligent bird species. Some species are known for imitating human voices. Parrots are interesting creatures not just for their intelligence and beauty but also for their diversity and variety. Not all parrots are the same; the diet, plumage, size, look and the vocal sound all differ from one type to another.

Squirrel

Squirrels are rodents that have long bushy tails. In many cities squirrels are seen more often than any other wild mammal. The squirrel family also includes groundhogs, prairie dogs, and chipmunks. There are more than 250 different animals in the family. Squirrels live on all continents except Australia and Antarctica. Tree squirrels make their homes in trees. Ground squirrels live in holes under prairies, deserts, and fields.

Guinea Pig

Guinea pigs are living beings with needs, wants and feelings just like you and I. They require time, money, love, understanding and commitment — over 5 years' worth in many cases. Having guinea pigs in your life can be a wonderful experience and very rewarding, but only if you think through your decision very carefully before you adopt and accept all that will be required of you as a guinea pig owner.

Canary

The canary is a small bird known for its singing. Canaries are found wild in the Canary, Azores, and Madeira islands off the coast of northwestern Africa. Hundreds of years ago people caught these wild birds and began breeding them. In the wild, canaries measure about 5.5 inches in length.

Gold Fish

The goldfish is a popular fish to keep as a pet in aquariums and ponds. It also lives in the wild, in rivers and lakes. The goldfish is a member of the carp family. Its scientific name is Carassius auratus. Goldfish are naturally greenish brown or gray. But about 1,000 years ago people in China began breeding goldfish to create more colorful varieties. There are now more than 125 breeds of goldfish found throughout the world.
