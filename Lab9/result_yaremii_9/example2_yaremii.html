~Care~


1. Never place mounts in direct sunlight.

2. Do not put mounts near heat, air conditioning vents or close to a working fireplace.

3. Let your friends know not to touch or pet the mounts. The salt and oils from their hands will make the hair brittle and cause it to break.

4. Clean the mounts with a feather duster. Always dust in the direction of hair growth when you are cleaning.
